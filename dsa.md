## 💡 How I Study DSA

1. **Attempt the problem first** — minimum 20-30 minutes before looking at hints
2. **Watch Striver's explanation** if stuck — understand the intuition, not just the code
3. **Code it yourself** — never copy paste
4. **Push to GitHub** — every solution goes up
5. **Revisit after 1 week** — spaced repetition is key
6. **Note the pattern** — most problems follow ~15 core patterns
